Adolescents in Therapy

Adolescence is a difficult time of life for many teens. It is estimated that approximately 1 in 5 teens have a mental health concern that impacts their school, work, or family and peer relationships. Many factors impact adolescent mental health, including genetic factors, family relationships, peer relationships, social pressures, body image, and trauma. Therapy can be a safe place for teens to process their thoughts and feelings. Renteria Counseling and Wellness engages adolescents by providing them with a comfortable, non-judgemental atmosphere to explore their concerns, while leveraging family values and strengths.
