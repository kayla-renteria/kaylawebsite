About Renteria Counseling and Wellness

Renteria Counseling and Wellness provides outpatient therapy to adults, children, and families dealing with a variety of concerns, including depression, anxiety, relationship difficulties, stress management, and overcoming the effects of trauma.

I provide counseling services in my office or via telehealth. Therapy services are individualized and tailored to each client's unique needs. I specialize in treating depression, anxiety, and trauma. I also offer a range of wellness services that compliment traditional counseling services, including meditation and yoga workshops, classes, and private sessions.
