Insurance and Rates

I currently accept Passport, Wellcare, United Health Care (sometimes called Optum), Cigna, Humana, and Anthem insurances. You’ll find my rates for services here:

Initial Intake Appointment, 55 min: $100

Individual Therapy Session, 55 min: $80

Family Therapy Session, 55 min: $90

Online Therapy Session, 30 min: $40

Online Therapy Session, 55 min: $80

If I do not accept your insurance, I am happy to create an invoice for you to submit to your insurance company for reimbursement through your out-of-network benefits. I also offer a sliding-scale fee for clients who meet financial criteria. I’d be happy to discuss payment options with you. Please call the office for more information.

Payment is collected at the time of service. Cash, check, credit card, health savings accounts and flexible spending accounts are accepted.
